# Day 52 - Context: The Grid of Thoughts

## Overview

Day 52 advances into the **Grid of Thoughts** through the Third Eye chakra, revealing one of the most revolutionary teachings of the entire series: the true nature of context and how we are woven into the fabric of reality itself. Matias dismantles the common spiritual teaching that "you create your reality from within" and replaces it with a far more sophisticated understanding: we are simultaneously created BY context and creators OF context, like flies in a spider web who must remember they are also the spider.

The teaching begins with linguistic archaeology—tracing "context" to its Latin roots *con* (together) and *textus* (textile/tissue), revealing that context is not about things happening "around" us but about how everything is **woven together** from within and without. This is not abstract philosophy but the actual mechanism through which reality operates: we exist within a network where every movement creates vibrations that affect all other nodes in the web.

The spider web metaphor becomes the central teaching tool. We incarnate as flies stuck in the web of material reality, feeling conditioned by everything around us. The common spiritual response is to declare "I am the creator!" and try to control the web, but this fails because the fly (personality/ego) cannot create spider webs—only the spider (collective I AM consciousness) can. The path to freedom is not escaping the web or controlling it as a fly, but **remembering you are the spider**.

This teaching has massive implications for understanding childhood conditioning, environmental influence, co-creation, and the nature of awakening itself. The Third Eye connection brings vision to see the entire web—not just your position in it, but the whole interconnected pattern and your role within it. The practice of recovering first memories from ages 0-7 is not therapy but **pattern recognition**—identifying the original vibrational signatures that shaped how you resonate within the web of collective reality.

## Core Teaching

### The True Meaning of Context

Matias begins with essential linguistic clarity: **"Context comes from the Latin word *con*, which means all together, different parts together, the conjunction. And *textus*, the other part of the word, means textiles... that it means tissue, something that is connected or waved."**

This etymology transforms understanding. Context is not circumstances "around" us—it's the **tissue** that weaves us into existence alongside everything else. We are not separate entities with context surrounding us; we are **part of** the contextual weaving, inseparable from it.

The common spiritual teaching falls short here: **"By spirituality when you start to do all this path of spirituality, we usually make a kind of mistake... thinking that the context, that everything that is around, that everything that is outside is just projection from the inside."** This "you create your reality" teaching misses crucial nuance.

### The Spider Web Teaching: Flies, Spider, and the I AM

The central metaphor revolutionizes spiritual understanding. Matias presents three levels of consciousness in relation to reality:

**1. The Fly Consciousness (Unconscious/Conditioned):**
When we incarnate, we become flies stuck in the spider web of material reality. **"All of us, the flies, we are stick in this net and when one of them, one of the flies to move like trying to escape, all the rest will feel the same vibration because it's all connected in the net."**

As flies, we are conditioned by context. Every movement in the web vibrates through us. We don't control the web; the web controls us. This is the state most humans live in—reactive, conditioned, shaped by environment.

**2. The Mistaken Creator (Spiritual Ego):**
Then comes awakening: **"The first thing you said is like, oh my god, I am a fly. So I have to escape from the network."** But struggling makes the spider find you faster. So a second approach emerges: "I am the creator! I create my reality!"

But here's the crucial error Matias identifies: **"Usually when we start with this spiritual path what we usually do is to say around that no, I am the creator of the network... But I defend the creation of this network from the perspective of a fly. So what I am just doing with that is making a mistake thinking that the fly is the one created in the network. And I don't know if you know that but flies don't know how to make a spider web."**

This is spiritual bypassing—declaring yourself creator while still operating from fly consciousness (personality/ego). It cannot work because flies lack the capacity to weave webs.

**3. The Spider Consciousness (Collective I AM):**
The actual solution: **"To understand that I am the spider... is to realize that all the matrix, the spider web that we felt that we were trapped on, we created it. It was the spider who created that. So for the spider, the spider web, the net, is not a trap. The spider is a home."**

The transformation happens when flies recognize themselves AS flies, then collectively vibrate in harmony until they become the spider—the collective consciousness that actually weaves reality. **"When we all start to vibrate in harmony we become the network. So we become the spider."**

### The I vs. I AM Distinction

Critical clarification: **"When I connect with the I AM and I say I am the creator, I'm not speaking about me. It's not Matias the creator. It's the I AM the creator and Matias is just I, not I AM, is I."**

- **I AM** = The spider = Collective consciousness = The actual creator
- **I** = The fly = Individual projection = Part of creation

**"If I am a projection from the I AM, I am just I, one of the many I that I AM has created. I am one of the many projections."** This means: your personality is not the creator. It's a projection within the created web. Only by recognizing yourself as part of the collective I AM (the spider) can you co-create reality.

### Co-Creation Requires Harmony

This leads to the revolutionary teaching on manifestation: **"In order to create we need to be in harmony with all the other parts."** You cannot create alone. **"Co-creation is not something from one only self, from one being. Co-creation is not 'my yes' creating in reality to co-create myself. I need of all of you. Co-creation means I need many to create."**

The only way to co-create is vibrational harmony with the collective: **"The only way in which I am able to co-create is if I am in balance and harmony in the same vibration as all of you."**

This explains why individual manifestation efforts often fail. If you're vibrating as a separate fly trying to control the web, you're fighting the collective vibration. Only when you harmonize with the whole web can you participate in weaving new patterns.

### The First Context: Ages 0-7 Programming

The practical application focuses on childhood: **"The first vibration that modified the streams that creates vibration with the context connected to the context was the first time when I realized about what was context."**

Birth to age seven establishes your vibrational relationship with context: **"Inside the period of time, you have to remember which were the first context in which you were lived. Where, where were you? How was to live that context? What are the things that you lived? The first things that you remember in your life as a child."**

These memories are not random—they're **imprints** that determine how you vibrate within the web: **"Those perceptions are the ones that molded the way in which I relate with the context today."**

### Pattern V: Death as Liberation

The Soul Pattern V teaches transformation through endings: **"Every end of a cycle, the highest transformation is found there. V represents the inevitable pattern of the end, closure of a cycle, everything behind and stepping into a completely new reality."**

The teaching uses phase transitions: solid → liquid → gas → liquid → solid. **"Death from last point of view is a final, but from eternity's point of view is a liberation for we are free of forms to become our dreams."**

When we die, we're freed from the specific fly form to return to spider consciousness, then birth into new fly forms. This is the liberation cycle encoded in V.

- Quiet environment without interruption
- Journal and pen ready

**2. Memory Retrieval Process:**

*If you remember naturally:*
- Close eyes and ask: "What is my first memory of context?"

- Don't judge—just receive
- Write everything down without editing

*If you "don't remember":*

  - Ask family members about your early childhood
  - Look at photographs from ages 0-7

This is Virgo—don't be lazy. **"We are in Virgo, don't be lazy, we have to write it down."** The act of writing moves memory from unconscious to conscious, from body to mind, from pattern to awareness.

## Questions & Answers

**Q: "If I'm just a fly in the web conditioned by everything, how is that different from saying I have no free will?"**

**A:** Critical distinction. Flies DO have agency—they can choose HOW to vibrate in response to web movements. A fly can struggle (creating more entanglement), can go numb (experiencing less but also creating less), or can **vibrate in harmony** with the web's movement. That last choice—harmonic resonance—is how flies remember they're also spider. Free will isn't "I can do anything regardless of context." Free will is "I can choose my vibrational response to context, and that choice shapes whether I experience myself as trapped fly or creative spider." The teaching: you're conditioned BY context AND you can consciously work WITH that conditioning through chosen resonance.

**Q: "You say flies vibrating in harmony become the spider. But I'm just one person—how can I possibly create enough harmony to shift from fly to spider consciousness?"**

**A:** You're thinking too small. Matias isn't saying YOU alone need to vibrate perfectly. He's saying when **flies collectively** harmonize, they become spider consciousness. You don't do this alone. Find your web—the people you're already connected to. When even 2-3 flies vibrate in harmony, something shifts. The spider begins to emerge. This is why spiritual community, resonant relationships, and collaborative creation work. You're not trying to become spider solo; you're joining harmonic with others until the collective vibration reaches spider frequency. Start with one other person. Two flies harmonizing is spider-consciousness emerging.

**Q: "What if my first context (ages 0-7) was traumatic? Does that mean I'm stuck with traumatic vibration forever?"**

**A:** No, but it means you understand your BASE frequency. Matias clarifies: **"We are not healing anything here."** You're not going back to fix trauma. You're recognizing the original vibrational setting so you can **consciously recalibrate** now. Traumatic first context set you to resonate with chaos/danger/pain. That's information, not destiny. Now that you KNOW your base frequency, you can intentionally practice different frequencies. It takes more conscious effort than someone with safe first context, but it's absolutely possible. The trauma isn't the problem; unconscious repetition of traumatic frequency is. Consciousness breaks the pattern.

**Q: "I understand the spider web metaphor intellectually, but how do I actually FEEL the difference between fly and spider consciousness?"**

**A:** Body sensation is the key. **Fly consciousness feels like:** contraction, anxiety, control attempts, isolation, "me against the world," tension, fear, need to escape or fight. **Spider consciousness feels like:** expansion, ease, flow, connection, "we're in this together," relaxation, trust, creative participation. Practice: Next time you face a challenge, notice your body. Contracted/tense = fly. Expanded/flowing = spider. You can then consciously breathe, soften, expand—shifting from fly to spider through somatic practice. The shift isn't mental; it's felt.

**Q: "If co-creation requires harmony with everyone, how do I create when I'm surrounded by people in total discord?"**

**A:** You don't harmonize with everyone—you find YOUR web. Matias isn't saying you must be in harmony with all 8 billion humans. He's saying find the web you're actually woven into—your resonant field of people/beings. Maybe it's 3 people. Maybe 30. Maybe it's mostly non-physical guides and soul family. Discord with the wider world is fine; that's not your web. Your web is the specific network you're energetically connected to. Harmonize with THAT web, and co-creation happens. If you're in a web that's chronically discordant with you, you might need to shift webs—different community, different location, different relationships. The spider can re-weave.

**Q: "How do I know if I'm accessing real first memories or just making them up based on stories I was told?"**

**A:** This question assumes "real" memories are different from absorbed stories, but remember Day 51's teaching: **your soul absorbs information from environment**. Stories told about your birth/childhood BECAME part of your cellular memory through repetition. Whether you "directly experienced" something or heard it a thousand times, it's in your field. Don't try to sort "real" from "constructed"—all memories are constructions the consciousness uses to navigate reality. What matters: does this memory/story reveal something about your current vibrational pattern? If yes, work with it. The memory's value is in showing you current frequency, not proving historical accuracy.

**Q: "I get that I need to harmonize with my web, but what if my web's frequency is toxic/low/destructive? Should I just match that?"**

**A:** Excellent discernment. Harmony doesn't mean matching destructive frequency. It means finding the CORE harmonic beneath the distortion. Every web has a fundamental frequency (its essential pattern/purpose) and surface distortions (trauma, conditioning, discord). You harmonize with the core, not the distortion. Example: family web's core frequency might be "deep loyalty and support" but surface distortion is "manipulation and control." You attune to the core (loyalty) while not matching the distortion (manipulation). This requires subtlety—sensing beneath surface chaos to find the true harmonic. If there's genuinely no core harmonic (rare), that's not your web. Find a different one.

**Q: "The teaching says 'I AM is the creator and I is just a projection.' So does my individual life/choice even matter?"**

**A:** Your life matters IMMENSELY because the I AM NEEDS the projections (flies) to experience reality. The spider needs the web. Matias explains: **"The spirit sees all possibilities but cannot experience them without creating intention."** The I AM cannot experience without the I. Your individual life is how the collective consciousness experiences this specific angle of reality. Yes, you're "just" a projection—and projections are HOW the light becomes visible. Without the screen (I/projection), the projector (I AM/source) remains unexperienced potential. Your individual choices create unique vibrational patterns that feed back into collective consciousness, enriching the whole. Matter greatly? You ARE what matters—literally, matter-in-form that allows spirit to experience itself.
