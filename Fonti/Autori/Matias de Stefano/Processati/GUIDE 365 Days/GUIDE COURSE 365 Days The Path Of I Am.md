## Tips for Alignment

It is best to be physically aligned with the planetary nodes and energy grids of the Earth while you do the alignment. As one of the options, you may go to a nearby church or temple as they are normally built along the energy grid.

Please refer to the energy grid map to find out which nodes and grids are nearest you.

If you cannot be close to those locations, do not worry. Just face the direction towards the closest node or grid located from where you are. **Your intention is the most important.**
