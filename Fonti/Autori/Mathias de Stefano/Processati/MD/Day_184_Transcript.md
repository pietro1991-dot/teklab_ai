## Core Teaching

### Vega and the Transition: Virgo to Leo

To understand Lyra, we must first understand what happened 12,000 years ago when Vega became the northern star and the world shifted from the **Era of Virgo** into the **Era of Leo**.

During the Era of Virgo, the **Atlantis civilization** spread around the whole planet. Atlantis was like a mother—it united beings that came from the stars with beings that were on Earth. These beings were not used to having the pigmentation of skin that comes from heavy oxygen exposure. They were very pale, almost transparent, and because you could see through their skin, you could see their veins—blue veins. That's why they were called **"the blue ones."**

The blue ones were the result of star beings (who had no pigmentation) mixing with Earth beings. You could see their blood vessels, and since their blood was not fully oxidized (they came from environments with less oxygen), they appeared very blue. Thus: **the blue ones**.

These blue ones were divided into **12 families**. The 12 families were organized this way because they had to go to the **12 main portals of the planet**—specific locations where Earth's energetic grid could be accessed and activated. By positioning themselves at these 12 portals, they could bring harmony to the planet's process and **open the doors of this spaceship** (Earth) through different dimensions.

### The Quest for Coherence

In order to open those doors, they needed to be the **perfect keys**. And to be perfect keys, they needed to be **coherent**.

**Coherence** means being aligned across all levels of your own self—**mentally, emotionally, and physically, aligned as one**. When your thoughts, your feelings, and your actions are in harmony, you create a coherent vibration. This coherence is what allows you to resonate with higher dimensions and access interdimensional portals.

The 12 families were trying to find this coherence so they could be in the correct vibrational frequency to make the planet resonate perfectly—to tune Earth like an instrument so it could receive and transmit cosmic information.

### Music as the Tool for Balance

The blue ones understood that **if everything is vibration, the best way to handle reality is through music**.

This is the connection to the Lyra constellation and the story of Orpheus. Orpheus played music so beautifully that he could calm demons and beasts. This is not just mythology—it's a memory of what the blue ones attempted to do. They tried to open the interdimensional doors of the world using vibration to **calm all the dark energies and distortions** from the universe.

**Distortions** are not "evil" in themselves—they are simply disharmonized frequencies. When something is distorted, it means it is **out of tune**. The goal is to find harmony, to balance the distortion so that the energy can flow freely and be used properly.

What the blue ones tried to do was find harmony in music, find harmony in structure, so that all the disharmonized and distorted realities could be balanced and freed within the network.

### The Earth as a Harp

The story of the lyre (the instrument) is very related to the story of the blue ones because **the Earth has this instrument in its electromagnetic field**. All the fields of the Earth look like the **strings of a harp**.

What the blue ones used to do was to try to **"play" the magnetic field of the planet** in order to find balance, harmony. They understood that Earth's electromagnetic field is a musical instrument—when you know how to touch it, how to vibrate it at the right frequencies, you can create harmony, open portals, and download information from other dimensions.

Remember from the previous teaching (Cygnus) that the 12 families went to live at the corners of the **dodecahedron structure**—the 12 portals of the planet. From those positions, they could handle the balance of the planet through vibration and music, so the magnetic field would work properly and the whole spaceship Earth could function.

### The Pyramids: Downloading Cosmic Information

What the blue ones started to do was find a way to **download information from every dimension through the magnetic field** by creating structures in this world that we call **pyramids**.

The pyramids are structures that **download the electrons of the electromagnetic field**—like lightning in a storm. They download mass amounts of energy from the environment and convert it into usable energy. They pull information from the magnetic field, from multiple dimensions, and make it accessible.

This network of pyramids created what we would call **free energy**—the ability to download electrons from the magnetic field, handle them, and share them with everyone. This is similar to what **Nikola Tesla** wanted to do with free energy—wireless transmission of power pulled from the Earth's own field.

The network that Atlanteans used is similar to what we use today with **Wi-Fi, internet signals, radio communications**—all the wireless technology we now depend on. The Atlanteans did the same thing, but they didn't use it to watch Netflix. They used it to **upload and download information from other realities and communicate between different regions of the planet and beyond**.

### The Requirement: Coherence (3-6-9)

The only way you can download that information is by being **coherent**—by connecting the three aspects of being: the **physical, transformative, and divine**.

In sacred geometry and numerology, this is represented by the codes **3, 6, 9**:
- **3** = the divine/spiritual aspect
- **6** = the transformative/soul aspect
- **9** = the physical/material aspect

This is the **architecture of the being**. When you align all three, you become coherent. And only coherent beings can access and transmit the high-frequency information that opens interdimensional portals.

Sit in silence for a few minutes. With your heart coherent, imagine you are a pyramid—a structure designed to download information from higher dimensions. What comes through? A thought? A feeling? An image? A knowing?

Trust what arrives. This is your download.

## Questions for Reflection

### Q: Who were the "blue ones" really?

Hybrid beings—part human, part star. Their star genetics gave them pale, transparent skin with visible blue veins (blood less oxidized than Earth humans). They were the children of Atlantis, the bridge between cosmic civilizations and Earth life. They carried knowledge of vibration, geometry, and coherence that they used to try to open Earth's dimensional portals.

### Q: What does it mean to be "coherent"?

Coherent means your thoughts, emotions, and actions are aligned—they're not contradicting each other. When you think one thing, feel another, and do a third, you create distortion (like static on a radio). When all three are in harmony, you create a clear signal that can interface with higher dimensions and access information. Coherence is the key to all advanced consciousness technology.

### Q: How did pyramids actually work as technology?

Pyramids are shaped to pull electrons from the electromagnetic field—like lightning rods for cosmic energy. Their geometry creates a standing wave that downloads information from multiple dimensions simultaneously. When positioned at Earth's grid points and activated by coherent consciousness, they become portals and free energy generators. This is what Tesla was rediscovering.

### Q: What is the relationship between music and reality?

Music is organized vibration. Reality is also vibration. When you play music, you are reorganizing the vibrational field around you. This is why certain music calms you, certain music energizes you. The Atlanteans understood this and used it deliberately to tune Earth's field, calm distortions, and open portals. Music is not entertainment—it's technology.

### Q: How can I use this knowledge practically today?

Become conscious of your vibration. Notice when you're coherent (aligned) vs. incoherent (scattered). Use music, breath, or meditation to tune yourself like an instrument. Recognize that your heart's electromagnetic field is broadcasting constantly—choose what signal you're sending. And practice downloading: sit in silence and receive information from the field.

### Q: What does "I Am Emissary of the Heart" mean?

You represent heart-centered consciousness in the world. Not emotional sentimentality, but **coherent, harmonious, balanced consciousness**. You are the one who brings distorted energies back into tune, who plays the harp strings of reality to create harmony. The galaxy speaks through your heart.

### Q: Why did Atlantis fall if they had all this knowledge?

Because knowledge without wisdom leads to destruction. They had the technology, but they lost the coherence. Power struggles, ego, misuse of energy—these created distortions they couldn't balance. The fall of Atlantis is a warning: Technology without heart-centeredness, without coherence, without ethics, will always self-destruct.
