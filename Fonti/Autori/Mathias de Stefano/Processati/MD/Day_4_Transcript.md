## Overview

On the fourth day of alignment, the focus shifts to the heart chakra, emphasizing the portal of heart and the transformation of energy through love and inspiration. The session guides participants to recognize and integrate emotions, release blockages, and open the heart to new experiences and higher vibrations.

## Core Teaching

### The Portal of Heart and Energy Transformation

The heart chakra serves as the portal for love, balance, and emotional transformation. Todayâ€™s teaching highlights the importance of acknowledging and integrating all emotions, including darkness and discomfort, as part of the self. By observing and understanding these energies, participants can transform and expand through love.

### Perception, Darkness, and Integration

The process of alignment may bring up feelings of discomfort, darkness, or heaviness. These are projections of inner energies being released and transformed. The teaching encourages participants to observe, contemplate, and integrate these aspects rather than reject them, understanding that nothing outside can harm unless allowed.

### Body Awareness and Preparation

Participants are guided to find a comfortable position, keep the spine straight, and honor the body through touch and massage. Breathing exercises and attention to sensations facilitate relaxation and readiness for meditation, allowing the body to download and integrate higher vibrations.

---

## Practice

### Preparation

1. Find a comfortable seated position with your spine straight, head pointed to the sky.
2. Massage and touch your body to honor and prepare it for alignment.
3. Relax your arms, legs, and hands, focusing on your breathing.
4. Breathe in through the nose, exhale softly, and become aware of your whole body.
5. Recognize areas of pain, discomfort, or pleasure without judgment.

### Main Practice Steps

1. Visualize three spheres: one for body, one for emotion, and one for idea, in front of your heart.
2. Contemplate the connection between these spheres and your heart chakra.
3. Bring the spheres into the core of your heart, nourishing it with their energy.
4. Visualize the light of your higher self descending through the crown, third eye, and throat to the heart.
5. Observe the light dissolving the spheres, feeding the seed within your heart.
6. Pronounce the vibration "Mi" as a mantra, feeling the seed open and blossom into a green flower.
7. Repeat the statement: "I Am the Portal of Heart" several times, integrating the energy.
8. Stretch, yawn, and discharge any remaining energy through your body and to the earth.
9. Gently open your eyes and return to the present moment.

### Closing

Rest and allow the energy of love and inspiration to settle within you. Discharge any residual emotions and prepare for the next day's alignment.
