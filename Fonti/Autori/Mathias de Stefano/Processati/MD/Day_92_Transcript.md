## Core Teaching

### Dreams: The Subconscious Language

Dreams are the way our subconscious expresses and talks to us constantly, showing what exists within. We ALL dream - those who say "I don't dream" simply don't remember. There's a difference.

We dream during specific phases when we're most relaxed. Usually we don't remember because we're waking up as the dream ends, transitioning back to conscious awareness too quickly to capture the dream content.

**Tool to remember dreams:** Drink lots of water 30 minutes before sleeping. Your body will wake you to use the bathroom during your deepest relaxation phases - exactly when you're dreaming. This natural interruption helps you catch dreams before they dissolve.

With time and practice, you'll connect and communicate with your subconscious more easily. Recording dreams is essential for this month's Scorpio work with the subconscious.

### NO MIND in Dream Work

Absolutely critical: Don't try to understand dream meanings. Don't interpret. Just write them down or record them. That's it.

The reason for "no mind" is simple: thinking about dream meanings puts mental goals on the dream, applying conscious interpretation to subconscious messages. To understand a dream through the subconscious (not the mind), we use intuition.

Let the dream come to you. Start communicating with it. No mind in both directions - receiving the dream and recording it. You'll have time later, when you feel called, to figure out meanings. But during Scorpio month, the practice is pure communication: allowing the dream, recording the dream, that's all.

This tool is for all Scorpio month because we're working with the subconscious. Dreaming is the best way to communicate with the subconscious because in that realm, all information within shows itself as it simply is. No mind, no interpretation - subconscious just shows up raw and unfiltered.

### Intuition: Not a Sixth Sense

We have these three water signs - Pisces, Cancer, Scorpio - the most intuitive of all twelve signs. Why? Think of sound vibration:

A high noise in air reaches maybe a kilometer, maybe a bit more. But in air, sound spreads out and disappears faster. In water, water pushes the waves forward. A whale or dolphin can communicate through many thousands of kilometers underwater.

Water carries vibration much farther and faster than air. So water signs perceive through their five senses at greater distance and subtlety than other signs. When we talk about intuition, we're not describing "sixth sense" or "seventh sense" - it's the FIVE senses refined to acute sharpness.

People very sensitive and intuitive don't have extra senses - they use the five senses at a different level of vibration. They can perceive things very high in vibration that others can't feel because those others' senses aren't refined enough yet.

### How to Activate Intuition

Many people ask: "How can I talk with my guides? How can I listen to them, see other realities, feel other dimensions?"

We fail because we think connecting to other dimensions requires different senses, a special gift. But nobody has a "gift" that others lack. We ALL have the same tools - the five senses. Some people simply use them refined, sharpened.

It's not "I don't have what they have." It's "They can perceive it and I can't YET, but I have the same tools." 

**How to refine your senses:** Practice with each one separately. Touch again and perceive reality with your touch much deeper. Smell better, really smell. Taste and enjoy the taste of things. Learn how to listen - truly listen. Learn how to see, to contemplate reality, colors, everything.

When you start using your senses much more refined, THEN you'll be able to feel all dimensions.

### The Piano Metaphor

Imagine each of 8 billion humans has a piano at home. Some people play lower notes, some higher notes. Some play rock, some classical. Some touch one note constantly. Some won't touch any notes at all. But we ALL have the same piano - we all have pianos.

You CAN learn to play the piano. We have it. The question is: will you practice?

Now imagine wanting to learn piano but having no idea which song to play or which notes come next. Different elements approach this differently:

- **Water signs:** They just FEEL it. They touch keys and it doesn't matter if it makes noise or sounds wrong - they just feel it. By feeling, they resonate with the song even before playing it. Eventually they know exactly what note comes next because they PERCEIVED it, not thought it.

- **Air signs:** Thinking constantly - am I doing it right? Am I wrong? Constant mental analysis.

- **Fire signs:** I want to hear it perfectly NOW. I want the right song, I want it immediately.

- **Earth signs:** Big book in front saying "This is A, this is B, A, B" - methodical, structured, learning by the manual.

Water signs flow and perceive - that's intuition. The ability to perceive what's coming next without expecting anything, just feeling it.

### The Meaning of Intuition

"Intuition" comes from Latin: "in" + "tueri" (to watch, to contemplate, to observe). "Intueri" means to be in the process of contemplation, to be in the process of observation.

Intuition is to observe yourself - not to take action, but to PERCEIVE. That's it.

The process of intuition takes us to the depth. Yesterday's channeled being showed us the higher self observing and watching everything. It can only watch, only see what's outside. But it cannot PRE-visualize.

To visualize is to see what's in front. To PRE-visualize is to close your eyes and know (by resonance, by vibration, by feeling) what action that thing will take. The only way to do this is by feeling and imagining.

Intuition is understanding that everything is connected in such a way that when you see a wave going out in the ocean, your feeling tells you: that wave is going to come back to me. And that's intuition - perceiving the return before it happens.

### How to Activate Intuition: Feel with Refined Senses

To activate intuition, feel with each of your senses much more refined. Practice perceiving with refined senses. Go into matter, into depth, into darkness where you can practice the senses.

Nature and the universe push and press these five senses so they can feel more and more. As much pressure you feel, the more you'll activate your ability to feel.

Awakening the potential of the senses requires PRESSURE. In ancient Rome, they called feeling lots of pressure being "sub-ferro" (under iron) - under very heavy iron receiving the pressure. Now we say "suffering."

We suffer in order to activate the potential of our senses. This is also described as Calvary (from Latin "calvarium" = skull) - going deep to the bone, to the core of feeling.

### Why Humans Suffer

Humans suffer NOT because the universe demands it for awakening potential. Suffering is a CHOICE, an option.

The reason humans suffer: instead of being who they're supposed to be (activating their own potential), they're constantly escaping from their potential, trying to live others' lives.

You run away from your own self trying to live others' lives - THAT'S suffering. Simple as that.

Whoever tries to escape from their own potential creates suffering in others. Example: Hitler wanted to be an actor, wanted to paint. Nobody allowed him. If he'd become an actor, maybe another history would be. 

Nobody can take you out from suffering. The only one able to take you out from suffering is your own ability to become more intuitive - to feel who you truly are and live that.

### Pressure Activates Potential

This concept of awakening sense potential through pressure: think of a seed under soil. The ground's pressure is what makes the seed explode upward, looking for sun, growing.

Everything beautiful in nature - crystals, flowers, all beauty we contemplate - was born because of pressure. Pressure of the atmosphere, pressure of tectonic plates, pressure of ground, rocks, gases. Beauty is born from pressure.

Everything humanity has accomplished that we call beautiful was because of pressure. Organic and inorganic beings (crystals, animals, us) all solve conflicts under pressure. This pressure creates conflict we need to solve, awakening the potential within to overcome that conflict.

The crystal has conflict solving magma's heat. Animals have the conflict of winter to survive. Seeds have the conflict of soil above. Everything in nature is pressure creating conflict we must solve - this is the tool for evolution.

## Q&A

**Q: What if I really don't dream?**
**A:** You DO dream - everyone does. You just don't remember. Try the water technique: drink lots 30 minutes before bed so your body wakes you during deep relaxation phases when you're dreaming. With practice, you'll start remembering.

**Q: Why can't we interpret our dreams?**
**A:** Because interpretation puts mental goals on the dream, applying conscious mind to subconscious messages. During Scorpio month, we're building pure communication with the subconscious - no mind involved. Later, when ready, understanding will come through intuition, not mental analysis.

**Q: How is intuition different from thinking?**
**A:** Thinking is mental analysis, working with known information, logical process. Intuition is perception - feeling what's coming before it arrives, knowing without knowing how you know. It's resonance, vibration, the five senses perceiving subtler levels than normal awareness catches.

**Q: Why are water signs more intuitive?**
**A:** Because water carries vibration farther and faster than air. A whale can communicate thousands of kilometers through water; sound in air only travels a kilometer or so. Water signs naturally perceive at greater distance and subtlety through their refined five senses.

**Q: Don't some people have special gifts others lack?**
**A:** No. We all have the exact same tools - five senses. Some people have practiced refining them, sharpening them to perceive subtler vibrations. It's like having the same piano - some practiced more, some less. But everyone has a piano.

**Q: How do I refine my senses?**
**A:** Practice each one separately. Really touch things, perceive textures/temperatures deeply. Actually smell consciously. Truly taste instead of just eating. Listen to distinguish sound layers. See colors/light/shadow with attention. Start treating senses like instruments to tune.

**Q: Why does suffering activate intuition?**
**A:** Pressure activates potential. Suffering happens when you escape your potential instead of living it - this pressure can force your senses to sharpen so you can finally perceive your truth. But suffering isn't required - conscious practice of refining senses works too, without the pain.

**Q: What's the goal for this month's dream work?**
**A:** Build communication with your subconscious. The subconscious shows information as it simply is - no mind, no interpretation, just pure showing. By recording dreams without thinking, you open that channel. With time and practice, this communication becomes easier and clearer.
