## Overview

We have explored dimensions 1 through 5. Today we enter the sixth dimension—perhaps the most challenging to explain because it exists beyond the perception of our five senses. The sixth dimension is the dimension of transformation, evolution, and infinite possibilities.

When Matias explains higher dimensions, he must transcend the vision of the third dimension to allow us to perceive them differently. In the fifth dimension, all perceptions—the five senses—connect themselves as one again. There is no division. That is why you might feel the words of your guides resounding in your chest instead of hearing them in your ear. It is all together as one.

The sixth dimension is like looking at a tree through a kaleidoscope. The tree suddenly has very weird shapes, divided and projected into many options. As you turn the kaleidoscope, the tree starts to turn upside down, the roots become branches, shapes replicate, becoming smaller and smaller. Suddenly it is no longer one tree but many options of that tree.

This is the bending and extending of time and space—the core function of the sixth dimension.

## Understanding the Sixth Dimension Through Dimensions

### The Foundation: Third, Fourth, and Fifth Dimensions

In the third dimension, we see a tree. Just the tree, one moment frozen in time.

In the fourth dimension, we see the whole history of the tree—the seed, the first leaf, the roots growing, the tree developing, the flowers, the fruits, all in one. We see all stages simultaneously. The reason we cannot normally see the whole process is because we live linearly through time, seeing only one stage at a time.

In the fifth dimension, we see the patterns that hold the tree. We see the energy that makes the tree exist. We understand the logic of why a tree exists—the consciousness that observes and holds the structure.

### The Sixth Dimension: The Kaleidoscope

In the sixth dimension, we see the tree as if inside a kaleidoscope. The tree divides, projects itself into many options. When we turn the image, the tree turns upside down, the roots appear where the crown was, shapes replicate, fractalize, becoming smaller and smaller.

The sixth dimension takes one thing and transforms it into thousands of variations, billions of possibilities. It is the dimension where one reality fractali

zes into countless parallel realities.

## The Constants: Time and Space

The universe is held together by two constants. Constant comes from the Latin meaning "the entity that stays together all the time." The two constants are time and space. They are always there.

In these constants, we have different crosses representing different times and different spaces. What determines the difference between these times and spaces? Integration.

According to the level of integration:
- A high vibration place we call "up"
- A low vibration place we call "down"
- A high vibration time we call "future"
- A low vibration time we call "past"

When time and space cross, they create a node—a singularity, a point where positive and negative aspects meet. This creates a magnetic field that discharges energy through the positive and absorbs through the negative. Because time and space are constants, the energy moves in a spiral, creating a spinning point like adjusting the strings of a guitar.

This spinning creates the bending of time and space. It curves the lines of time and space, making nodes that were in process bend toward specific regions, creating gravitational centers like suns, stars, and planets moving in orbits.

This is how the third dimension appears in the constants of time and space.

## The Sixth Dimension: Transformation and Evolution

The sixth dimension is the dimension of the architects of the universe—the one that designs and transforms all things. It takes a concept, a data, an object from the third dimension and transforms it into a different thing, a new reality, again and again.

We do not have direct examples in the third dimension to see the sixth dimension because it is impossible to perceive fully. The only way we can summarize it is through evolution. Evolution is the sixth dimension, but we see it through the vision of the third and fourth dimensions, which takes billions of years. In the sixth dimension, you can see the whole process in one moment.

### The Example of the Letter "A"

When you are born, you say the sound "A" when you cry. Later, you learn to say words like "mama" or "dada." Then someone teaches you the letter A, and you write it. Then you learn to read words with A. Later you forget about the letter and think only about the content of the text. Eventually you get a degree, maybe a PhD, at 25 years old.

Now when you think about "A," you think about concepts of wisdom and knowledge, not the letter itself.

The sixth dimension does exactly this with reality. It takes the letter A from the third dimension and transforms it into a word. It takes the word and transforms it into a concept. It takes the concept and transforms it into a text. It takes the text and transforms it into poetry.

The sixth dimension is the one transforming one thing, fractalizing it, making it more and more complex. Just as from one tiny letter we can create millions of books on different topics, the universe from one tiny particle can create all of this. The sixth dimension allows this fractalization.

## Chirality: The Mirror That Does Not Match

The sixth dimension is a polar, dual dimension. This duality is what we call chirality. Chirality means that two equal things are opposite. Like hands—they are exactly the same, but when you put one over the other, they do not fit. Two fingers stick out.

This explains why there are thousands of options of parallel realities in the sixth dimension. Even though they are equal to us, they do not match with us, so we cannot interact with them.

This has a logic in the universe. In the sixth dimension, they found a way to keep moving and not get stuck in reality. This is by creating a polarity in which both realities love each other eternally but can never fully match. So they look for billions of ways to try to match.

This is the cold, geometric way to explain twin souls and twin flames.

### Twin Souls: Complementary Flames

The design of reality is born from the conjunction of time and space. It is the spark of a fire that you can see this way or that way. These two flames are opposite one another, which means they are complementary.

Each one needs to help the other find the path to connect with the other side. These flames must multiply, fractalize themselves thousands of times in different directions until they find the path to activate the flame exactly like the other, matching with the other.

All along this path, trying to find the connection between the soul and the spirit, what are all the points in between? They are fractalization trying to reach unity. These are people—people I myself created, and people who created me. We are all reflections of the attributes of our soul and our spirit.

That is why we fall in love with other people, believing they are our twin souls or twin flames. I am not saying you should not look for your twin soul. I am not saying you should only love yourself. Everyone is yourself. The entire universe is yourself. You can fall in love with everyone, looking for the attributes of yourself.

The only thing I am saying is that we should rethink how we live this concept. The sixth dimension creates thousands of billions of options of one reality. To handle the creation of our own self, we must allow ourselves to love every option we have created. Love the others as if they were yourself, because that is exactly what they are.

## Q&A

### Q: If there are infinite parallel realities in the sixth dimension, why can I not access them?

Because they are chiral—equal but opposite. Like your left and right hands, they do not fit together perfectly. They exist in the same dimension but cannot interact because they are mirror images that do not match.

### Q: Does this mean my twin soul is impossible to find?

Not impossible, but misunderstood. Your twin soul is not one person waiting for you. It is the complementary flame of your being, fractalized into countless expressions. Every person you love carries an attribute of that flame. The journey is not to find "the one" but to recognize yourself in all.

### Q: How can I use the sixth dimension practically in my life?

By understanding that one choice, one idea, one action can fractalize into thousands of outcomes. You are not limited to one path. The sixth dimension shows you that transformation is always possible. What you are today can become countless things tomorrow.

### Q: What is the relationship between the sixth dimension and evolution?

Evolution is the sixth dimension seen through the slow time of the third and fourth dimensions. In the sixth dimension, you see the whole evolutionary process instantly—how one cell became billions of species, how one idea became entire civilizations.

### Q: If I am creating parallel realities in the sixth dimension, am I living multiple lives simultaneously?

In a sense, yes. But you, as the third-dimensional being, experience only one timeline linearly. The sixth dimension holds all possibilities, but your conscious awareness is focused on this one reality. Other versions of you exist in the kaleidoscope, but you cannot directly access them while embodied here.

### Q: What does it mean that the sixth dimension is the dimension of architects?

It means the sixth dimension is where reality is designed and transformed. Architects take one blueprint and create variations, improvements, new structures. The sixth dimension does this with existence itself—taking one pattern and fractalizing it into infinite creative expressions.

### Q: How does this relate to the sacral chakra and creation?

The sacral chakra is the center of creativity and transformation in the body. The sixth dimension is creativity and transformation in the cosmos. When you create—art, life, relationships—you are accessing sixth-dimensional energy through your sacral center, transforming one thing into many, birthing new possibilities.
