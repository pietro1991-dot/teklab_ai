# TEKLAB PRODUCT MATRIX - Complete Product Line Overview

## OIL LEVEL REGULATORS (TK Series)

### TK3+ Family - Electronic Oil Level Regulator
**Base Technology**: Infrared electro-optic sensor, microprocessor control, no moving parts

**Pressure Variants**:
- **TK3+ Standard (46 bar)** - Standard refrigeration systems with HFC, HFO refrigerants
- **TK3+ MP (Medium Pressure, 60-80 bar)** - Medium pressure applications
- **TK3+ HP (High Pressure, 90 bar)** - High pressure systems
- **TK3+ CO2 (130 bar)** - CO2 transcritical systems (R744), specifically designed for high differential pressure

**Connectivity Variants**:
- **TK3+ Base** - Relay output only, no digital communication, standalone installations
- **TK3+ MB (MODBUS)** - MODBUS RTU RS485 protocol for BMS/SCADA integration and centralized monitoring

**Key Features**:
- IR optical sensor with prism technology (no moving parts, no wear)
- Integrated alarm relay for active safety
- Operating temperature: -40°C to +125°C
- Fast response time (instantaneous detection)
- Programmable timers to filter transients
- "Install and forget" reliability
- Compatible with all modern refrigerants (HFC, HFO, CO2, NH3)

**Typical Applications**:
- Compressor oil level regulation
- Refrigeration systems (commercial and industrial)
- Heat pumps and chillers
- Large retail distribution (supermarkets)
- Cold storage facilities

---

### TK4 Family - Advanced Oil Level Regulator with NFC Configuration
**Base Technology**: Same IR optical technology as TK3+ PLUS NFC wireless configuration

**Pressure Variants**:
- **TK4 Standard (46 bar)** - Standard refrigeration
- **TK4 HP (90 bar)** - High pressure systems
- **TK4 CO2 (130 bar)** - CO2 transcritical applications

**Connectivity Variants**:
- **TK4 Base** - NFC configuration + relay output
- **TK4 MB (MODBUS)** - NFC configuration + MODBUS RTU RS485 + advanced diagnostics

**Unique Features vs TK3+**:
- **NFC wireless configuration** via smartphone app (no need to power the device)
- **Advanced diagnostics** with historical data logging
- **Parameter adjustment** without opening electrical panel
- **Remote troubleshooting** capabilities
- All TK3+ features included

**When to Choose TK4 over TK3+**:
- Multiple units to configure (NFC speeds up commissioning)
- Need advanced diagnostics and predictive maintenance
- Want smartphone-based parameter adjustment
- MODBUS + comprehensive diagnostics required
- Higher initial investment justified by maintenance savings

---

### TK1+ - Sight Glass Replacement Controller
**Single Model Configuration**:
- **TK1+ (46 bar)** - Direct replacement for mechanical sight glasses

**Specialized Application**:
- Replaces traditional glass level indicators on compressor crankcases or oil reservoirs
- Provides electronic safety vs. passive glass observation
- Compact design for tight installation spaces
- Same reliability as TK3+ family

**Key Features**:
- Ultra-compact footprint
- Relay alarm output
- Eliminates glass breakage risk
- Visual + electronic monitoring
- Easy retrofit on existing compressors

## LIQUID LEVEL SENSORS (LC Series)

### LC-PS Family - Standard Pressure Optical Switch (46 bar)
**Technology**: Infrared optical detection, digital ON/OFF output

**Model Variants**:
- **LC-PS Cable** (3-wire pre-wired cable) - Standard installation with cable
- **LC-XT M12** (M12 industrial connector) - Same functionality as LC-PS with removable connector for easier maintenance

**Technical Specifications**:
- Maximum pressure: 46 bar
- Compatible refrigerants: HFC, HFO, R134a, R404A, R410A
- Universal output: NPN/PNP/AC in single model (no need for separate SKUs)
- Protection: IP67
- Operating temperature: -40°C to +125°C
- Response time: Instantaneous (<1 second)

**Key Advantages**:
- Excellent cost-effectiveness (vs mechanical floats)
- No moving parts (vs. float switches)
- High accuracy (±2mm detection point)
- Works with viscous oil, foam, turbulence
- Universal output reduces BOM complexity

---

### LC-PH Family - High Pressure Optical Switch (120 bar)
**Technology**: Infrared optical with reinforced structural design for extreme pressure

**Model Variants**:
- **LC-PH Cable** (3-wire cable) - 120 bar, for CO2 transcritical systems
- **LC-XP M12** (M12 connector) - 120 bar, industrial connector version

**Technical Specifications**:
- Maximum pressure: 120 bar (specifically for CO2 transcritical)
- Compatible refrigerants: CO2 (R744), HFC, HFO, high-pressure applications
- Reinforced machine body and materials for extreme mechanical/thermal stress
- Same universal output as LC-PS
- Rotalock adapter compatibility (eliminates need for connection adapters)

**When to Choose LC-PH over LC-PS**:
- CO2 transcritical systems (mandatory choice)
- Operating pressure >46 bar
- High-pressure NH3 applications
- Need for reinforced construction in demanding environments

---

### K25 - Dual Sensor (Level + Temperature in One Unit)
**Technology**: Combined IR optical level sensor + PT1000 temperature sensor

**Single Model**: K25 (integrated dual sensing)

**Revolutionary Innovation**:
- **Replaces 2 separate sensors** (level + temperature) with one device
- **50% installation time reduction** (one mounting vs two)
- **Single leak point** instead of two (increased system safety)
- **4-20mA temperature output** (no need for PT1000 signal converter)
- Factory calibrated (level + temperature)

**Technical Specifications**:
- Level sensing: IR optical (same as LC series)
- Temperature sensing: PT1000 integrated
- Temperature output: 4-20mA analog signal
- Pressure rating: 46 bar (standard version)
- Single M12 connector for both signals

**Cost-Benefit vs Separate Sensors**:
- Installation: 50% time savings
- Leak points: Reduced from 2 to 1 (safety improvement)
- Cable management: Simplified (one cable vs two)
- Commissioning: Faster (one device to configure)
- Maintenance: Single spare part

**Typical Applications**:
- Systems requiring both level and temperature monitoring
- Evaporator monitoring (level + superheat temperature)
- Condenser applications (level + subcooling)
- BMS integration requiring both measurements

### ATEX-Certified Sensors (Explosive Environments)
**Safety Certification**: ATEX intrinsic safety for explosive atmospheres

**Available Models**:
- **LC-PS ATEX** (46 bar) - Standard pressure with ATEX certification
- **LC-PH ATEX** (120 bar) - High pressure with ATEX certification

**Critical Applications**:
- **Mandatory for flammable refrigerants**: R290 (propane), R600a (isobutane), R1270 (propylene)
- **Ammonia systems** (NH3/R717) in hazardous zones
- **Explosive atmosphere classifications**: Zone 1, Zone 2 (ATEX directive compliance)
- **Safety-critical installations** where intrinsic safety is required by regulation

**Key Compliance**:
- ATEX Directive 2014/34/EU
- Intrinsic safety (equipment cannot cause ignition)
- Non-negotiable for flammable refrigerant installations
- Required for insurance and regulatory approval

## PRODUCT SELECTION DECISION TREE

### Question 1: Do you need OIL LEVEL REGULATION or just LEVEL DETECTION?

**If REGULATION (automatic oil return control)**:
→ Go to TK Series (TK1+, TK3+, TK4)

**If DETECTION only (level switch, ON/OFF signal)**:
→ Go to LC Series (LC-PS, LC-PH, LC-XT, LC-XP) or K11/K25

---

### TK Series Selection: TK1+ vs TK3+ vs TK4

**Choose TK1+ if**:
- Replacing mechanical sight glass on compressor crankcase
- Need compact footprint for tight spaces
- Application: Oil reservoir visual + electronic monitoring
- Budget: Most economical TK option

**Choose TK3+ if**:
- Need reliable oil level regulation with "install and forget" simplicity
- Budget-conscious vs TK4 (no need for NFC diagnostics)
- Single-unit or few installations (NFC benefit minimal)
- Standalone systems without BMS (TK3+ Base) OR centralized monitoring (TK3+ MB)
- Proven technology, lower cost than TK4

**Choose TK4 if**:
- Multiple units to configure (NFC saves commissioning time)
- Need advanced diagnostics and historical data logging
- Want smartphone-based parameter adjustment (no panel opening)
- Predictive maintenance strategy (TK4 MB with diagnostics)
- Higher initial cost justified by maintenance savings and troubleshooting ease

---

### LC Series Selection: LC-PS/XT vs LC-PH/XP

**Choose LC-PS or LC-XT if**:
- Operating pressure ≤46 bar
- Standard refrigeration (HFC, HFO refrigerants: R134a, R404A, R410A)
- Simple level detection (not regulation)
- Cost-effective solution vs mechanical floats
- **LC-XT variant**: Prefer M12 connector for easier maintenance/replacement

**Choose LC-PH or LC-XP if**:
- Operating pressure >46 bar (up to 120 bar)
- **CO2 transcritical systems** (mandatory choice, 120 bar required)
- High-pressure NH3 applications
- Need reinforced construction for extreme conditions
- **LC-XP variant**: M12 connector for maintenance convenience

---

### Specialized Sensor Selection

**Choose K11 if**:
- OEM equipment manufacturer (chillers, heat pumps)
- Need universal output compatibility (NPN/PNP/AC single model)
- Compact size is critical
- Want to simplify BOM and inventory (one part for all PLCs)
- Global supply chain (works with any PLC brand)

**Choose K25 if**:
- Need BOTH level AND temperature measurement
- Want to reduce installation time (50% vs 2 separate sensors)
- Single leak point preferred (safety/reliability)
- 4-20mA temperature output required (vs PT1000 converters)
- Streamlined commissioning (one device to configure)

**Choose ATEX-certified sensors if**:
- **Mandatory**: Using flammable refrigerants (R290, R600a, R1270, NH3)
- Installation in explosive atmosphere zones (Zone 1/2)
- Safety certification required by regulation or insurance
- Non-negotiable for legal compliance

## REFRIGERANT COMPATIBILITY MATRIX

**All Products Compatible With**:
- HFC refrigerants: R134a, R404A, R407C, R410A
- HFO refrigerants: R1234yf, R1234ze, R513A
- Natural refrigerants: CO2 (R744), NH3 (R717)
- HCFC refrigerants: R22 (legacy systems)

**Pressure-Specific Requirements**:
- **R744 (CO2) transcritical**: MUST use 120-130 bar models (TK3+/TK4 CO2, LC-PH/XP)
- **R290, R600a (flammable)**: MUST use ATEX-certified models for safety zones
